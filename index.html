Our Classes

Group Fitness Classes:

Aerobics and Dance Classes: High-energy classes set to music, often involving dance or choreography. Step Aerobics: Involves stepping up and down from an elevated platform to improve cardiovascular fitness. Zumba: A dance-based workout that combines Latin and international music with dance moves.

Strength and Conditioning Classes:

Body Pump or Group Weight Training: Incorporates weightlifting and strength training exercises in a group setting. Bootcamp: Intensive workouts combining strength, cardio, and agility exercises for a full-body workout.

Mind-Body Classes:

Focuses on flexibility, strength, and relaxation through various postures and breathing techniques. Pilates: A low-impact exercise method that focuses on core strength, flexibility, and overall body conditioning.

Stretching Training

Involves holding a stretch for an extended period, typically 15-60 seconds. This helps improve flexibility and is often done after a workout.
